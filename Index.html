ayasbit.com takes some time to load the informations but if the loading takes longer , it might be due to an improper internet connection or an unsupported web browser , latest version of chrome is recommended .

In order to represent , the current price has increased or decreased from the last price the signs have been placed "+" represent increased ( or unchanged ), "-" represent decreased .

The "+" , "-" signs beside the exchanges balance shows has the current total balance increased ( or unchanged ) or decreased from the initial balance since the capture started .

The sent and recieved are the total quantity of BTCs sent or recieved reapectively since the capture has been started from the particular exchange .

A slow internet connection causes this irregularity , As the Ayas Bit converts price on the basis of the latest updated price .

Ayas Bit shows decimal places upto two places except the BTC / USD conversion to mantain the usefulness of the infornations shown in it .
